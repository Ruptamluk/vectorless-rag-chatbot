# Acme Robotics — Business Travel Policy

## Booking Travel
All business travel must be booked through the Acme travel portal at least 14
days in advance when possible. Economy class is standard for flights under 6
hours; premium economy is permitted for flights of 6 hours or more. Rail travel
is preferred over flights for trips under 300 km.

## Accommodation
Hotel stays are capped at $200 per night in standard cities and $300 per night
in designated high-cost cities (New York, London, Tokyo, San Francisco). Book a
standard room; upgrades are at the traveler's own expense.

## Meals and Daily Allowance
Travelers receive a per-diem of $60 per day for meals and incidentals, or may
submit itemized receipts up to the same daily limit. Alcohol is not reimbursable.
The per-diem does not apply on travel days where meals are otherwise provided.

## Ground Transport
Use ride-share or public transit where practical. Rental cars require manager
approval and must be economy or mid-size. Mileage for personal vehicle use is
reimbursed at the current standard rate.

## Expense Submission
Submit all travel expenses through the expense portal within 14 days of
returning. Expenses over $200 require manager approval, consistent with the
general expense policy in the Employee Handbook. Reimbursement is processed
within two pay cycles.
