# Acme Robotics — Pricing and Subscription Plans

## Hardware Pricing
The Acme R1 robot is sold outright at $18,500 per unit. Volume discounts apply:
5% off for orders of 5–9 units, 10% off for 10–24 units, and 15% off for 25 or
more units. The docking/charging station is $1,200 and one is recommended per
two robots.

## Fleet Console Subscription
Acme Fleet Console is licensed per robot, per month:

- **Starter** — $40/robot/month: live monitoring, task assignment, email support.
- **Professional** — $75/robot/month: everything in Starter plus the REST API,
  webhooks, and WMS connectors.
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing: Professional features plus SSO, dedicated
  support, a 99.9% uptime SLA, and on-prem deployment.

Subscriptions are billed annually. Switching plans takes effect on the next
billing cycle.

## Support Tiers
Starter includes email support with a 2-business-day response target.
Professional adds chat support with a 4-hour response target during business
hours. Enterprise includes 24/7 phone support with a 1-hour response target for
critical issues.

## Trial and Refunds
A 30-day free trial of the Professional plan is available for new customers.
Hardware may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, minus a
10% restocking fee, provided it is undamaged.
