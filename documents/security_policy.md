# Acme Robotics — Information Security Policy

## Passwords and Authentication
All employee accounts must use a password of at least 14 characters and enable
multi-factor authentication (MFA). Passwords are rotated every 12 months or
immediately after any suspected compromise. Shared accounts are prohibited.

## Data Classification
Data is classified into three tiers: Public, Internal, and Confidential.
Confidential data — including customer records and robot telemetry — must be
encrypted at rest and in transit, and may only be accessed on managed devices.

## Device Management
Laptops must run full-disk encryption and the company endpoint agent. Lost or
stolen devices must be reported to security@acme.example within 24 hours so the
device can be remotely wiped.

## Incident Response
Suspected security incidents must be reported to the security team immediately.
The incident response team aims to acknowledge reports within 1 hour and
contain confirmed incidents within 4 hours. A post-incident review is completed
within five business days.

## Third-Party Access
Vendors requiring system access are granted time-limited, least-privilege
credentials. All third-party access is logged and reviewed quarterly.
