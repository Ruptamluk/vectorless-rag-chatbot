# Acme Robotics — Product FAQ

## What is the Acme R1?
The Acme R1 is an autonomous warehouse robot designed to move payloads of up to
50 kg across flat indoor surfaces. It navigates using lidar and onboard cameras
and does not require floor markers or magnetic strips.

## Battery Life and Charging
A fully charged R1 runs for approximately 9 hours of continuous operation. It
charges to 80% in about 45 minutes using the docking station and supports
opportunity charging — it can top up during idle periods automatically.

## Safety Features
The R1 has a 360-degree obstacle-detection field and stops within 15 cm when a
person or object enters its path. An emergency-stop button is located on the top
panel, and the robot enforces a maximum speed of 1.5 m/s in shared human zones.

## Software and Integration
The R1 fleet is managed through Acme Fleet Console, a web application. It exposes
a REST API and supports webhooks for task completion events. Integrations with
common WMS platforms are available via the Acme connector library.

## Warranty
Every R1 ships with a 2-year hardware warranty covering manufacturing defects.
Battery packs are warranted separately for 12 months or 800 charge cycles,
whichever comes first.
