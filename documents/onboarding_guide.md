# Acme Robotics — New Hire Onboarding Guide

## Your First Day
New hires should arrive at the main office at 9:30 AM on their start date. Bring
a government-issued photo ID for badge creation. Your manager will meet you in
the lobby and walk you through your team introductions and workspace setup.

## Equipment and Accounts
IT provisions a laptop, monitor, and accessories before your start date. You
will receive your account credentials by secure email and must set a password
and enable multi-factor authentication on day one. See the Information Security
Policy for password requirements.

## First-Week Checklist
- Complete the mandatory security awareness training (about 45 minutes).
- Read the Employee Handbook and acknowledge it in the HR portal.
- Meet 1:1 with your manager to agree on 30/60/90-day goals.
- Set up your remote-work arrangement if applicable (see Remote Work policy).

## Mentorship Program
Every new hire is paired with a mentor from a different team for their first 90
days. Mentors hold a weekly 30-minute check-in and help you navigate Acme's
tools, culture, and processes.

## Probation Period
The standard probation period is 90 days. A formal review is held at the end of
probation to confirm the role. Feedback is shared continuously, so there should
be no surprises at the review.
