# Acme Robotics — Employee Handbook

## Working Hours and Flexibility
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Acme
operates a flexible-hours policy: employees may start any time between 7:00 AM
and 10:00 AM as long as they complete 8 hours and are present during the core
collaboration window of 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote
arrangements require director approval and are reviewed every six months.
Remote workers must remain reachable during core hours and keep their
calendars up to date.

## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 public
holidays. Vacation accrues monthly and up to 5 unused days may be carried into
the next calendar year. Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days annually.

## Parental Leave
Acme offers 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for the primary caregiver and
6 weeks for the secondary caregiver, available within the first year after birth
or adoption.

## Expenses and Reimbursement
Work-related expenses under $200 can be self-approved and submitted through the
expense portal. Anything above $200 requires manager pre-approval. Reimbursement
is processed within two pay cycles.
